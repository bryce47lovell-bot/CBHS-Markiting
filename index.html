About the Program

The Marketing Program at Cherokee Bluff High School teaches students how businesses promote, sell, and manage products and services. Students gain hands-on experience through projects, teamwork, and community involvement.

Courses

Marketing Principles

Learn branding, customer behavior, pricing, promotion, and business decision-making fundamentals.

Marketing & Management

Explore how businesses plan, organize, and operate, while building leadership and project skills.

Entrepreneurship

Learn to start and run a business, develop marketing plans, and pitch your ideas.

DECA

DECA is a national student organization that enhances marketing skills. CBHS DECA members:

  • Compete in business, marketing, and entrepreneurship events
  • Attend conferences and workshops
  • Work on community projects
  • Develop leadership and presentation skills

Why It Matters

The Marketing Program prepares students for careers in business, entrepreneurship, sales, sports marketing, social media, advertising, management, and communications. Even for students not pursuing business, the program builds communication, teamwork, and critical thinking skills.
