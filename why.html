Real-World Skills

The Marketing Program teaches students valuable skills used in every industry, including communication, teamwork, leadership, and critical thinking.

Career Preparation

Students gain experience that prepares them for careers in business, advertising, social media, entrepreneurship, and marketing. The program also strengthens college applications and resumes.

Soft Skills Developed

  • Effective communication
  • Team collaboration
  • Problem-solving and decision-making
  • Leadership and initiative
  • Creativity and critical thinking

Success Stories

Graduates of the CBHS Marketing Program have gone on to successful college programs and careers in marketing, sports & entertainment, advertising, and entrepreneurship.

Summary

The program provides a comprehensive education in marketing, leadership, and business. Students graduate prepared to excel in college, careers, and real-world business environments.
