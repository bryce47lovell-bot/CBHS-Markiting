Program Overview

The Marketing Program at Cherokee Bluff High School provides students with practical skills in business, marketing, and management. Students engage in hands-on projects, real-world applications, and leadership activities to prepare for college and careers.

Mission

Our mission is to develop confident, knowledgeable students who can analyze, plan, and execute marketing strategies while demonstrating leadership and teamwork.

Program Benefits

  • Hands-on marketing experience
  • Preparation for college and career pathways
  • Opportunities to participate in DECA competitions
  • Leadership development
  • Collaboration and teamwork skills

Future Opportunities

Graduates of the program gain a solid foundation for careers in marketing, advertising, sales, sports & entertainment marketing, and entrepreneurship. The program also strengthens college applications and resumes.
