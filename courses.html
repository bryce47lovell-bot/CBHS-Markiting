Course Overview

The CBHS Marketing Program offers a sequence of courses designed to teach students business fundamentals and marketing skills. Each course includes hands-on projects, real-world applications, and opportunities for leadership.

Courses Offered

Marketing Principles

Students learn about branding, customer behavior, pricing, promotion strategies, and how businesses make marketing decisions. Projects include designing advertisements and planning marketing campaigns.

Marketing & Management

This course teaches students how to plan, organize, and operate businesses. Students develop leadership and project management skills while working on group projects and real-world scenarios.

Entrepreneurship

Students learn how to start and run a business, create marketing plans, pitch ideas, and understand business operations. This course encourages creativity, problem-solving, and business strategy skills.

Skills Developed

  • Marketing analysis and planning
  • Leadership and teamwork
  • Project management
  • Communication and presentation skills
  • Creativity and problem-solving

Student Projects

Each course includes hands-on projects that simulate real-world business challenges. Students create marketing campaigns, manage events, and collaborate in teams to present solutions.
